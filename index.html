About General Emilio Aguinaldo Water District

The General Emilio Aguinaldo Water District (GEAWD) is committed to delivering safe, clean, affordable, and reliable water services to every household and business in the Municipality of General Emilio Aguinaldo, Cavite.

Through quality service, transparency, and continuous improvement, GEAWD aims to provide sustainable water supply for present and future generations.
